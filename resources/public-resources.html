    Gold Standards Survey

    This fall, the subcommittee is admistering its inagural annual survey. The survey will go live on September 21, 2020-watch your inbox for an email from our Co-Presidents with the official announcement.

    Each participating institution should only submit ONE Qualtrics survey, even if the data collection process involves multiple stakeholders across your institution, completed to the best of your ability.


    FINAL FINANCIAL METRICS SURVEY SUBMISSION

    Link to a Qualtrics survey that you will complete once you've collected as much data as you're able to. Please use your Gold Standards Financial Metrics Collection Tool to aid in survey completion. This survey should only be completed by an institution price.

    GOLD STANDARDS FINANCIAL METRICS SELECTION TOOL

    An Excel workbook for you to use to store data as you collect it. This is merely a tool to aid in consolidation of data and completion of the official Qualtrics survey-it is not being turned in.



    Gold Standards Financial Metrics Definitions
    Document containing definitions of the various financial terms and metrics found within the survey

    Letter from the Co-Presidents to NACRO Members
    The letter sent out to all NACRO members on September 21, 2020, introducing the survey.

    Letter from the Co-Presidents to University Leadership
    A letter for instructions to use as needed when presenting the survey to leadership.

    Establishing Gold Standards for Financial Metrics in Corporate Relations
    One-pager detailing the "What, Why, How, Impact, and Next Steps" of the survey.

    The Value of Establishing Gold Standards for Financial Metrics in Corporate Relations
    Value statements from leaders in research and advancement on the importance of the survey.

    Gold Standards Financial Metrics Survey Frequently Asked Questions
    Frequently asked questions regarding the survey for use as needed.
